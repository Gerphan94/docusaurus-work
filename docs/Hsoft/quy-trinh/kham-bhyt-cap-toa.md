## Step:

1. Chọn `4.Khám bệnh` >> `4.15 Đăng ký khám bệnh mới`

![image](https://github.com/Gerphan94/docusaurus-work/assets/81787710/f029c312-6db5-4f18-87a4-ee9e42c3ac88)
